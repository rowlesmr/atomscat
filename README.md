Creates a CIF file detailing the Inverse Mott-Bethe coefficient fo the atomic scattering factors of all elements, most cations, and a couple of anions.

Coefficients come from Thorkildsen, G. (2023). Acta Cryst. A79, 318-330 and Thorkildsen, G. (2023). Acta Cryst. A79, 318-330 (https://doi.org/10.1107/S2053273323003996 and https://doi.org/10.1107/S2053273323010550).

Original data from Olukayode, S., Froese Fischer, C. & Volkov, A. (2023). Acta Cryst. A79, 59-79 and Olukayode, S., Froese Fischer, C. & Volkov, A. (2023). Acta Cryst. A79, 229-245 (https://doi.org/10.1107/S2053273322010944 and https://doi.org/10.1107/S205327332300116X).

There is a new paper out, and once those data are reduced, this will be updated with those (Copeland, H., Watanabe, Y. & Volkov, A. (2026) Acta Cryst. A82, 200-217; https://doi.org/10.1107/S2053273326003554)
Raw data available: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20289472
